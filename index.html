Leadership

Top innovators define their innovation purpose clearly and set aspirational objectives that align with the overarching strategy. They take deliberate choices about innovation opportunities, ie. areas where innovation will create the biggest value for the organisation and superior impact. They focus on a limited number of opportunities in which they can leverage and build a unique strategic capability. Leadership drives this innovation agenda from the top, allocating resources according to strategic innovation priorities.
